# Mare Nostrum

## Tagline
Build your Roman city, raise an army, conquer the Mediterranean.

## Description
Mare Nostrum is a strategy game set in the ancient Mediterranean. Start as a shipwrecked Roman exile, build your island into a thriving city, recruit legionaries, and conquer 27 islands across 8 faction territories.

### Features
- **8 Unique Factions**: Rome, Carthage, Egypt, Greece, Persia, Gaul, Phoenicia, Sea People
- **27 Islands**: Trade hubs, military forts, resource islands, faction capitals
- **Visual Combat**: Watch your soldiers fight on the actual island terrain
- **Diplomacy**: Form alliances, trade agreements, or declare war
- **City Building**: Expand from a village to a metropolis (25 levels)
- **Multiple Victory Paths**: Military (conquer 6 capitals), Economic (trade empire), Diplomatic (alliance network)

### Controls
- WASD — Move
- E — Interact / Enter buildings
- F — Invade enemy islands
- L — Army management
- X — Expand city
- M — World map
- TAB — Empire dashboard
- ESC — Pause
