Buildings must use warm Mediterranean colors (cream, terracotta, warm sandstone) not cool greys. Doors should be warm wood brown (not black voids). Windows need warm shutters and golden night glow.

**Why:** User explicitly called the buildings "creepy" — the grey tones and black door interiors made them look abandoned/haunted rather than cozy.

**How to apply:** When creating or editing building sprites, use the warm palette: wall base 210-225/192-208/162-178, doors 85-110/50-68/20-28, window glow at night (255/200/80 with alpha). Buildings should spawn in district clusters with shared floor tiles, torches/lanterns between them, and road connections. Never spawn buildings in isolation.
