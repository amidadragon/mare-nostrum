**Emotional throughline (the "three fires"):**
- Era 1 campfire = personal survival
- Era 2 forum eternal fire = civic warmth
- Era 3 crystal beacon = mythic permanence
The player lit the first fire. They built the last one.

**Three eras:**
- **Era 1 (Lv 1-8): The Village** — Castaway camp to farming settlement. Palette: warm earth, amber, ochre. No roads, just worn-earth paths. Four NPCs on daily domestic loops. Wow moment: first evening with all four NPCs home by the campfire.
- **Era 2 (Lv 9-17): The City** — Roman civitas with districts. Paved Via, aqueduct, forum, castrum, lighthouse. Ambient citizen NPCs (8-12). Wow moment: standing at the temple top seeing the whole city below you.
- **Era 3 (Lv 18-25): Megalopolis / Atlantis** — Crystal-powered solarpunk-Roman hybrid. Ring channels + bridges, floating elements, crystal towers, beacon. Wow moment: first night the ring channels fully glow — visible from the wreck beach.

**The Atlantis Ring System (Level 18+):**
- Three concentric water channels fill with crystal-lit teal water
- Ring 1 (Sacred, r~120px): pyramid/beacon, Oracle pool, near-silence
- Ring 2 (Civic, r~260px): forum, library, bathhouse, arena
- Ring 3 (City, r~400px): market, castrum, residential, harbor
- Bridges: Roman arch + crystal keystone, 6 total
- Night: all ring water glows teal, pulsing slowly

**Key continuity rules:**
- The campfire NEVER disappears — it becomes the forum eternal fire, then the crystal beacon. Same emotional object, three forms.
- The dock's rough wood is NEVER fully upgraded. It's where everything began.
- The cats are in every era, sleeping in the most beautiful spot available.
- The four NPCs' faces never change. Their civic roles evolve.

**Distance visibility (Era 3):** Beacon pulse visible from other islands. Home island silhouette has teal base glow at night. This is the strongest feedback in the game — player looks back from another island and sees their own light.

**Why:** Grand vision session to define the island's transformation as the primary emotional arc of the game. The "exile to Atlantis" arc IS the game's identity.

**How to apply:** All island expansion decisions should serve these three era transitions. Any building, landmark, or terrain feature should be classifiable into one era's palette and mood. Never add Era 3 elements before Level 18 — the transformation must feel earned.
